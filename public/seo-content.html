Powerful Image & PDF Editor

Go beyond simple cropping. Our full-featured editor turns your browser into a creative canvas for images and PDF documents. It's the perfect tool for quick annotations, corrections, and creative compositions.

  • Comprehensive PDF Support: Open multi-page PDF documents and edit them page by page. Add signatures, text, and annotations to contracts, forms, and reports with ease.
  • Advanced Drawing Tools: Use the Pen tool for freehand drawing or the Eraser tool for corrections. Both tools feature a full color palette, an Eyedropper to pick colors directly from the image, and adjustable thickness.
  • Object-Based Editing: Every element you add—be it a line drawn with the pen, a shape, or text—is an independent object. Select, move, resize, rotate, and delete any element at any time.
  • Sign Documents Digitally: Add your signature by drawing it on our signature pad or by uploading an image of your existing signature. Place it anywhere on your images or PDF pages.
  • Annotation Tools: Add Arrows, Rectangles, and Circles to highlight important areas. Customize their color, thickness, and transparency.
  • Text Tool: Insert text with full control over font family, size, and color. Perfect for watermarking or adding captions.
  • Easy Navigation: The Hand tool allows you to effortlessly pan around the canvas, while the mouse wheel lets you zoom in and out for pixel-perfect precision. Canvas movement is automatically disabled when a drawing tool is active to prevent mistakes.
  • Transformations: Quickly Flip your images horizontally or vertically, and access a powerful Crop & Resize tool for exact dimensions.

Professional Icon Generator

Stop resizing images one by one. Our Icon Generator takes a single source image and instantly creates a complete set of icons for any platform, saving you hours of tedious work.

  • Pre-configured Profiles: Get all the sizes you need with one click for Favicon/Website, Progressive Web Apps (PWA), and Chrome Extensions.
  • Custom Sizes: Need a specific dimension? Simply enter any width and height to generate icons tailored to your project.
  • Intelligent Cropping: Start with a perfectly centered square crop or use the entire image as your source.
  • Ready-to-Use Zip Files: Download a single, organized .zip file containing all your generated icons, and for some profiles, even the necessary manifest files.

Versatile Image Converter

Convert images between the most popular web formats quickly and efficiently. Our converter is built for both single-file adjustments and large batch operations.

  • Multiple Formats: Convert to and from PNG, JPG/JPEG, WEBP, GIF. You can even convert images into a PDF document.
  • Batch Processing: Drag and drop up to 30 images at once to convert them all to your desired format in a single operation.
  • PDF to Image: Upload a PDF and convert its pages into high-quality JPG or PNG images.
  • Integrated Editing: When working with a single image, you can access the editor to resize or make adjustments before converting.

Custom QR Code Generator

Make your QR codes stand out from the crowd. Our generator lets you create beautiful, branded, and highly functional QR codes that capture attention and invite scans.

  • Full Design Control: Customize the color of the dots, corners, and background. Choose from various styles for both the dots and corners to match your brand's aesthetic.
  • Add Your Logo: Upload your company logo or any other image to be placed in the center of the QR code.
  • Include a Caption: Add a short, readable text caption directly below your QR code—perfect for a call-to-action or a brief description.
  • Multiple Data Types: Create QR codes for URLs, plain text, vCard contacts, Wi-Fi network credentials, calendar events, geographical locations, and more.
  • High-Resolution Export: Download your final QR code as a high-quality PNG (at various sizes) or a scalable SVG vector file.
